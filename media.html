ADA Accessibility
Wheelchair Friendly
View Transcript

We have an ADA-accessible charter buses upon request with special lifts, accessible restrooms, and widened aisles. It is important that we can get everyone safely to our destination. We take full responsibility in making sure you are safe and comfortable.

Comfort
Spacious Legroom
View Transcript

A comfortable ride is a promise. When you ride with us, we offer a luxury experience. All our seats have extra legroom and reclining seats. The whole trip comes with WiFi, bathrooms, USB power outlets, and individual back seat screens.We offer our guests unlimited amount of snacks and drinks.

Safety
Routine Safety Inspection
View Transcript

All our buses have been safety inspected. We check our engines, keep our buses up to date, check our wheels, and deep clean everything before a trip. We also make sure we have our first aid stocked up.

Technology
GPS Tracking
View Transcript

We offer real-time bus tracking so you always know where your bus is and your estimated time of arrival. This is great for so many reasons, such as group coordination, being able to keep everyone in the know of where the bus is and the estimated time of arrival.

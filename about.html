About Us

Why Hire Us?

We are a local charter bus company in the DMV area. We provide quick quotes and can work with any schedule. SuperTours guarantees safe transportation and is always on time. In addition, we hire local experienced bus drivers when demand is high. All our buses have luxury seating and have recently been updated. SuperTours charter buses feature cushioned seating, TVs, free headphones, bathrooms, and ADA accommodations. We have over 20 years in this business and believe charter buses are a great way for group events. Many companies have hired us for work trips, as well as large families looking to go on vacation. We guarantee that the ride will be pleasant, comfortable, luxurious, and affordable!

Welcome

Hello, my name is Darwin. I have been driving charter buses for over 20 years. I have done everything in my career from short distance trips to long service weeks. My favorite thing about my job is being able to connect with people and help others. If I had one motto in life it would be to "always be on time." We respect and value the customer above all else. I started this business many years ago because I believe the American dream is possible. I was born in Honduras and immigrated to the USA in search of a better life. I work hard every day for my children and their future. When you support SuperTours Charter Bus Services, you support not only a small business but a father. Thank you!
